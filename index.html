Tea Farm Record Keeping App

Streamline your tea farm management with BetFam, the comprehensive record-keeping solution designed for tea farmers.

Key Features:

  • Green Leaf Tracking: Easily record and monitor daily green leaf kilos.
  • Employee Wage Management: Calculate and track wages based on performance.
  • Statistical Insights: View monthly and yearly charts for production analysis.
  • Employee Performance: Monitor individual employee kilo contributions.
  • Cloud Storage: Securely store all data on each user's Google Drive.

BetFam simplifies tea farm operations, helping you make data-driven decisions to improve productivity and profitability. Whether you're managing a small plantation or a large estate, BetFam adapts to your needs, providing a user-friendly interface for efficient record-keeping.
